About Us

Welcome to StudyMate – Your Smart Study Companion!
StudyMate is an AI-powered chatbot developed specially for students of Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Government Polytechnic College.
Whether you need help with syllabus topics, exam preparation, previous year question papers, college notices, or quick tech support – StudyMate is always here to assist you.

    What StudyMate Can Do:

  • Answer your academic questions anytime, anywhere
  • Share class schedules, exam timetables, and syllabus details
  • Guide you through practicals, assignments, and project ideas
  • Help you explore career paths and learning resources
  • Connect you with important college updates instantly

Why Use StudyMate?

We understand that students need quick, reliable, and personalized support. StudyMate saves your time by providing instant help – just like a friend who’s always ready to assist.
